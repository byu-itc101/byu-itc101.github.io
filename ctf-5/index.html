CTF #5 – Binary and Hexadecimal

Challenges

Complete these picoCTF tasks on data representation:

  • Glitch Cat
  • convertme.py
  • binhexa

Overview

These problems practice converting between binary, decimal, and hexadecimal and working with basic bitwise operations. Focus on clean conversions and careful reading of each prompt.

General Tips

  • Know how ASCII values map to hexadecimal.
  • Be comfortable converting binary ↔ decimal ↔ hexadecimal.
  • Some operations are simpler in decimal—switch bases when it helps.
  • When in doubt, write it out or use a quick converter to verify.

Binhexa Tips

  • Binary Number 1 does not mean 0001; read the prompt carefully.
  • Left shift vs. right shift: moving bits can change length (a left shift may produce a 9-bit result).
  • Use OR (|) and AND (&) as defined; no extra tricks.
  • Double-check each step before converting back to the requested format.

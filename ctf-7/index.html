Wireshark

  • A packet capturing software.
  • In CTFs, forensics/networking will most likely be using WireShark.
  • You will have to download WireShark in IT&C 247.
  • In Class Demo of Packet Primer.
  • Macs: If you have an M1 or M2 Mac, download the ARM version of Wireshark. If it is older, download Intel version.

Pcap Poisoning

  • We know what all flags have… now how do we search for it??
  • It is a string.
  • For the flags, they might have spaces in them. When you type your flag into PicoCTF, type it in without spaces.

Homework 5 – Evaluating and Citing Cybersecurity Information

Submission template: assets/HW5.docx

Overview

This assignment develops your ability to evaluate cybersecurity sources critically and cite them appropriately. You will analyze multiple source types and reflect on how credibility, bias, and evidence quality affect cybersecurity decision-making.

Required Library Activity

Visit the Harold B. Lee Library site, open the Books/eBooks section, and locate Safari Tech Books (O'Reilly). Sign in with your byu.edu account if needed. Search for Security+ SY0-701 by Sari Green and watch any video from any module.

Take notes. You will use them in Source 4.

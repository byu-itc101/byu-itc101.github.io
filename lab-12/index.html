IT&C 101 LAB DISCOVERY ASSIGNMENT

Report

Submission template: assets/Lab12.docx

PURPOSE:

Cybersecurity requires a broad knowledge of computing skills that involve a variety of software and hardware. The term “Information Technology” is used differently by those in the profession, the news media, and others; the same is true of “Cybersecurity”. The purpose of this assignment is to find out what an actual expert thinks about IT&C.

LAB PROCEDURE:

  • Your interview will focus on the basics of Cybersecurity and what it is. Interview one person that works in Cybersecurity.
  • Interview the person using the questionnaire (included at the end of this assignment sheet) about their opinions on IT or cybersecurity and how they use these terms, but you can also include any questions that you feel are pertinent to the conversation.
  • As you interview the participants, record the person’s responses on a copy of the questionnaire.
  • Summarize your findings in your lab report.
  • Please be respectful to the professional who is giving you the interview. Their time is valuable and should not be wasted.

FORMAT:

Fill out the questions from the list below and then write a summary of what you learned beneath. Write it so that the reader of this report should be able to easily identify the following formatting criteria:

  • Who wrote the report?
  • What is the purpose of your study?
  • Summarize the results for each of the open-ended questions (found at the end of the questionnaire).
  • Describe several things you learned from this lab.
  • Include a summary of what you learned
  • Include the questionnaire with the individual responses in an appendix
  • Format your lab report in such a way that the reader can quickly go to a specific question to read.

Job Title:

  • How did you start your career in cybersecurity, and what steps did you take to get to where you are now?
  • What certifications or degrees do you consider essential or beneficial for someone entering the field?
  • What technical skills do you think are most important for a cybersecurity professional to master?
  • How do you stay updated with the latest cybersecurity threats and trends?
  • What other information technologies do you want to know about or use but don’t currently know about or use?
  • What are the biggest challenges you face in your role, and how do you overcome them?
  • What do you find most rewarding about working in cybersecurity?
  • How do you see the field of cybersecurity evolving in the next few years?
  • Are there any specific areas within cybersecurity that you think are particularly promising or in demand?
  • How does cybersecurity relate to other computing majors (e.g., Computer Science, Information Systems, Computer Engineering, Software Engineering, and Information Technology)?
  • How important is teamwork and collaboration in cybersecurity projects?
  • What forms of communication do you use and how important is it to your job?
  • How do you approach the ethical challenges that arise in cybersecurity?
  • Can you share an example of a situation where ethical considerations played a significant role in your decision-making?
  • What do you think people who work in “Information Technology” or “Cybersecurity” do?
  • What advice would you give to someone just starting out in cybersecurity?
  • Are there any books, online courses, or other resources you would recommend for someone new to the field?
